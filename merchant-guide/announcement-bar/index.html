    Editing the Announcement Bar¶

    Header

    What it changes: The thin promo strip at the very top of every page (e.g. "FREE SHIPPING ON US ORDERS 175+"), including its optional countdown timer.

    Where to find it: Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize → in the left sidebar open the Header group → click Announcement Bar.

    Editing the Text¶

    01 Open the Header group and select Announcement Bar.

    02 Edit Announcement Text and adjust the text/background colors.

    03 Click Save.

    Adding a Countdown¶

    01 Open the Header group and select Announcement Bar.

    02 Tick Is Countdown Enabled?.

    03 Set Date and Time Before Drop using the exact format mm/dd/yyyy hh:mm:ss (e.g. 11/30/2025 23:59:00).

    04 Adjust the Time Block text/background colors if needed, then click Save.

    Date format matters

    The countdown date is a plain text field — it must be typed exactly as mm/dd/yyyy hh:mm:ss. A mistyped date will make the timer blank or stuck.
