# Editing the "Notify Me" Waitlist Modal

<span class="bla-badge">Popup</span>

**What it changes:** The "Notify Me When Available" popup (waitlist / restock signup) —
its title, colors, and the connected Klaviyo form.

**Where to find it:** Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize → select the
template where it appears (**Product**, **Home page**, or **Collection**) → click the
**Join Waitlist Modal** section.

## Steps

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">01</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Select the **Join Waitlist Modal** section on the relevant template.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">02</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Paste your **Klaviyo Form ID** (the short random code from the Klaviyo form editor).</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">03</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Edit the **Modal Title Text** and the background/backdrop colors.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">04</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Click **Save**.</span>
</div>

!!! warning "It needs a Klaviyo Form ID"
    Without a valid **Klaviyo Form ID**, the modal won't capture signups. On the
    product-page copy of this section, leave **Is Modal for Product Page?** enabled so
    it uses the product layout.
