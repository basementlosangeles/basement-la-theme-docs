# Editing the "Styled By The Community" Panel

<span class="bla-badge">Product</span>

**What it changes:** The "Styled By The Community" scroller on product pages — its
heading, the "no items" fallback text, and how far the arrow buttons scroll. (The
photos themselves are blocks.)

**Where to find it:** Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize → switch the
template to a **Product** page → click the **Community Side Panel** section.

## Steps

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">01</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Open a **Product** template and select the **Community Side Panel** section.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">02</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Edit the **Title** (default "Styled By The Community").</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">03</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">To add or change photos, expand the section and edit its **Community Item** blocks.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">04</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Click **Save**.</span>
</div>
