# Editing the Footer

<span class="bla-badge">Footer</span>

**What it changes:** The site footer — its menus, the "Get Early Access" email signup,
the logo link, the copyright line, and footer colors.

**Where to find it:** Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize → click the
**Footer** section in the left sidebar.

## Steps

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">01</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Select the **Footer** section.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">02</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Choose the **Footer Main Menu** and **Footer Social Menu** from your saved menus.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">03</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Toggle **Enable Early Access?** and edit its label/placeholder, then update the **Copyright Text**.</span>
</div>

<div class="bla-step" markdown>
<span class="bla-step__num">04</span>
<span class="bla-step__body">Click **Save**.</span>
</div>

!!! note "Menus are managed separately"
    The links inside the footer menus are edited under **Shopify Admin → Content →
    Menus**. Here you only pick *which* menu appears.

!!! warning "Copyright year is manual"
    The copyright text doesn't update automatically — change the year here each January.
